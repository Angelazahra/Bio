Biography

Angela Zahara, born in the UK and raised in Syria, has always been captivated by the arts. She started with classical piano, earning a degree in music from the Institute of Music in Damascus while still in high school. Later, she pursued acting, graduating at the top of her class with a Bachelor's degree in Dramatic Arts from the Higher Institute of Dramatic Arts, University of Damascus.

Her career in the Middle East included over 15 major productions, where she worked with top talents and earned a large fan base. After moving to the UK, she took on roles in internationally recognized projects like *Journey to Mecca* and *Three Veils*, winning Best Lead Actress Award at the New York City International Film Festival in 2011.
